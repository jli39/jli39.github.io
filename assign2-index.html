Portrait of Eleanor of Toledo

The portrait of Eleanor of Toledo and Her son is a painting by the Italian artist Agnolo di Cosimo, known as Bronzino. finished ca. 1545. one of his most famous works, it is housed in the Uffizi Gallery of Florence. Italy and is considered one of the preeminent example of Mannerist portraiture.
